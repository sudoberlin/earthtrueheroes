About Us

This is what We do.

We are developing game/education app for children for ages (5-12) that would not only teach them conseravtion of resources but also develop an instinct that they would hopefully follow throughout their life and become earth's true hero.

The app would present different scenarios to the children and ask to choose an action that they would take in those situations, We also present facts about the climate change and how a small contribution could impact the world on the large scale.

The app will also consist of educational component where children would learn about the measurement of resources consumed or conserved along with QnA. The content will vary according to the age of the child tand their progress toward developing a responsible mindset.

Currently we have developed very basic prototype that shows a scenarios/action game play.
